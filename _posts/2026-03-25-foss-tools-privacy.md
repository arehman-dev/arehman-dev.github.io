A collection of free and open-source software tools essential for digital privacy, security, and freedom in the modern era.

## Essential Privacy Tools

- **KeePassXC** — Open-source password manager with local storage
- **Mullvad VPN** — Privacy-focused VPN with no logging
- **Signal** — End-to-end encrypted messaging for everyone
- **Tor Browser** — Anonymous web browsing
- **ProtonMail** — Encrypted email service
- **Thunderbird** — Privacy-first email client
- **Firefox** — Open-source browser with privacy focus

## System & Security

- **Linux (Fedora/Debian)** — Open-source operating system
- **Nextcloud** — Self-hosted cloud storage
- **LUKS** — Full disk encryption
- **GnuPG** — Cryptographic software

These tools empower users to maintain control over their digital lives without relying on proprietary services with questionable data practices.
