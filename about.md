# About

Assalamu alaikum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh.

I am **A. Rehman**, an I.T. professional and digital craftsman committed to the pursuit of knowledge (*al-ʿIlm*) and conscious servitude (*ʿubūdiyyah*). My life is anchored in traditional virtues, ethical computing, and the relentless pursuit of *Iḥsān*—excellence in every endeavor.

## Background

With years of experience in system design, backend infrastructure, and open-source communities, I channel my technical expertise toward building tools that serve humanity rather than exploit it. I believe technology should reflect objective morality and human dignity.

- **Craftmanship** — Treating every line of code and written word with care
