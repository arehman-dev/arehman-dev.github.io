# About

Assalamu alaikum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh.

I am **Abdul Rehman**, known in digital space as _a.rehman_. A student of knowledge pursuing a career in I.T. I occasionally write here, share visuals from my lens, and reflect on art that resonates with me.

## Journey Through Life:

My journey spans many transitions: from college to career, from photography to writing, from contemporary sciences to Islamic scholarship, from Western influence toward traditional family values.

## Interests & Values:

**What guides my life**: ʿubūdiyyah (servitude), family, love, the pursuit of knowledge, code, strong coffee, bikes, and strength training.

I endeavor to embody objective morality, Ḥayāʾ (modesty), and Iḥsān (excellence) as my guiding principles.
